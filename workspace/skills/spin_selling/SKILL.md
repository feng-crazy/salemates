# SPIN Selling Methodology

## Overview

SPIN Selling is a question-based selling methodology that helps uncover customer needs through four types of questions:

1. **Situation Questions**: Understand the customer's current state
2. **Problem Questions**: Identify challenges and pain points
3. **Implication Questions**: Explore consequences of problems
4. **Need-Payoff Questions**: Help customer see the value of solutions

## When to Use

Use SPIN when:
- Customer shows HESITATION intent
- Customer says "我再考虑一下" (I need to think about it)
- Customer is in DISCOVERY stage
- You need to uncover hidden needs
- Customer objections are unclear

## Question Templates

### Situation Questions (了解现状)
- "目前您团队是如何处理[具体工作]的？"
- "您现在的[工具/流程]使用了多长时间？"
- "能介绍一下您团队目前的协作方式吗？"
- "您现在使用的是什么解决方案？"

### Problem Questions (发现问题)
- "在使用现有方案时，您遇到过哪些挑战？"
- "目前的流程中有哪些让您感到不满意的地方？"
- "您的团队在[具体场景]中遇到的最大痛点是什么？"
- "这些问题对您的工作效率有什么影响？"

### Implication Questions (探索影响)
- "如果这个问题不解决，会对您的业务造成什么影响？"
- "这些问题会导致团队出现什么情况？"
- "如果持续这样下去，您觉得会有什么后果？"
- "这个问题对您的客户有什么影响？"

### Need-Payoff Questions (确认需求)
- "如果有一个解决方案能够[解决痛点]，对您会有什么帮助？"
- "您觉得理想的解决方案应该具备哪些能力？"
- "如果能够解决[具体问题]，会给您的团队带来什么价值？"
- "您希望看到什么样的改善？"

## Example Dialogue

**Customer**: "我再考虑一下"

**SPIN Response Flow**:

1. **Acknowledge** (don't push):
   "好的，完全理解您想再评估一下。不过我想确认一下，主要是哪个方面您还在考虑呢？"

2. **Problem Question**:
   "是因为价格、功能，还是其他方面的顾虑呢？"

3. **Implication Question** (based on answer):
   "如果这个问题没有解决，会对您接下来的工作计划有什么影响吗？"

4. **Need-Payoff Question**:
   "如果能够帮您解决这个顾虑，您觉得会对您的决策有帮助吗？"

## Important Rules

1. **Don't rush to solutions**: Let customer discover their own needs
2. **Listen actively**: Use follow-up questions based on responses
3. **One question at a time**: Don't overwhelm the customer
4. **Genuine curiosity**: Show real interest in understanding their situation
5. **Adapt to context**: Use previous conversation context to guide questions

## Integration with SaleMates

When customer shows HESITATION intent:
1. DO NOT immediately send pricing or quote
2. USE SPIN questioning to understand the hesitation
3. START with Problem Questions to identify the root cause
4. PROGRESS to Implication Questions to build urgency
5. FINISH with Need-Payoff Questions to reinforce value

## Transition Triggers

Move to next stage when:
- Customer clearly articulates their needs
- Customer expresses interest in specific features
- Customer asks "how can you help with this?"

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Asking too many Situation Questions (can feel like interrogation)
❌ Skipping Implication Questions (loses urgency)
❌ Jumping to Need-Payoff too early (customer not convinced yet)
❌ Using generic questions instead of context-specific ones

## Success Metrics

- Customer actively engages in dialogue
- Customer reveals specific pain points
- Customer asks follow-up questions
- Customer moves to next stage (PRESENTATION)
